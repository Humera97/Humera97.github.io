Presidential Candidates and Google

The 2020 Presidential election is still more than a year away, yet politicians have already begun campaigning and debating. But from the top Democratic candidates, who are we trying to learn more about? And what does that mean?

Humera Lodhi

Published October 2019

Who gets Googled the most?

On average, Bernie Sanders is searched the most-- both in the United States, and globally. However, there are a few notable incidents when Joe Biden's surges have surged, and Elizabeth Warren recieved more searches than the othe two candidates during the late debate.
